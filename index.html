Our Story

Welcome to Panwar Mojari, your trusted family-owned footwear shop in the heart of Jodhpur, Rajasthan. For decades, we have been crafting exquisite hand-made mojaris, juttis, shoes, and sandals that blend traditional Rajasthani artistry with modern comfort.

Located in the historic Mochi Market near Siwanchi Gate Road, Bhistiyon Ka Bas, we take pride in preserving the art of traditional Rajasthani craftsmanship.
